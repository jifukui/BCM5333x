Getting started with Rate Limit and Storm Control

Rate Control determines the bandwidth of ingress and egress traffic for a specific port.

Port indicates the port number. Click the port number to control ingress and egress rates for the port.

Ingress Rate indicates the rate limitation of incoming traffic in this port.

Egress Rate indicates the rate limitation of outgoing traffic in this port.

Storm Control indicates the storm control status. Click the hyperlink of the status for further storm control configuration.
