Getting started with Port-Base QoS setting

Port-Base QoS operation sets the priority relationships between four queues, selects the scheduling method for these queues, associates packets of specific priorities to specific queues, and {1,2,4,8} "weight" for mapping from low to high queue.

Scheduling Method specifies one scheduling methods (Weighted Round Robin) for the queues.

Queue [0:3] specifies four queues. Queue 0 is the lowest priority queue, queue 3 is the highest priority queue. Packets in queue 3 are served more often than packets in queue 0.

Ports specifies each of ports

Weight indicates the weight (number of packets) to be served in the queue before moving to serve next queue. A high priority queue should have a higher weight than a low-priority queue.
